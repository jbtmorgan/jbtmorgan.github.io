Jayden Morgan's Project Portfolio - School Projects

Throughout highschool and university, I have tackled many different projects consisting of vastly different difficulties.


Rolling Ball Structure

In ENGR121, I was required to work in a team of 3 people to design, test, and build a rolling ball structure. My contribution was to deliver an electrically controlled speaker, gate-system, and an elevator that raised a 1" steel ball one meter high. The most difficult part of it was finding the time to construct it while still doing well in my other courses.


3D Printed Bridge

In ENGR112, each student proposed a design for a bridge challenge. I am fortunate enough that I was able to interest to test my design as I own a 3D printer already. It was a fun project as I shared the provided video with my classmates demonstrating that my proposed design could hold a substantial amount of weight.
