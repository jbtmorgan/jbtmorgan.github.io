Components of Nissan 350z Adapter Kit

About the design:

The photos provided show a transmission plate as well as a flywheel-crank-adapter for a Nissan 350z. The end use of this kit is intended to connect a Nissan 350z Transmission to a Volkswagen Turbo Diesel Motor. The first photo (transmission plate) contains the profile of the transmission, a hole for the starter motor, and a method to connect to the Volkswagen side of the kit. The second photo (crank-adapter) is the method of connecting a 350z flywheel to a Volkswagen motor.

Tail kit for Motorcycle

About the design:

A common modification to make to a motorcycle is to buy an aftermarket tail kit to have brighter lights, as well as make the rear end look more aesthetically pleasing. To reduce costs, I made a concept sketch and 3D printed a prototype of my custom tail kit. It held the lights license plate effectively and once I was satisfied with the fitment it was then made from metal to withstand the conditions. This project consisted of modelling, metal work, and electrical work.
